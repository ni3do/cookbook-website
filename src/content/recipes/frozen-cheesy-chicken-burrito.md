## Ingredients

- `400g` boneless skinless chicken thighs
- `1` taco seasoning pack
- `2` limes
- `200ml` chicken bouillon
- `400g` green enchilada sauce or pelati (crushed tomatoes)
- `100g` rice
- `1` jalapeno, diced
- `1` onion, diced
- `1 bunch` cilantro, chopped
- `160g` Greek yogurt
- `50g` cheddar cheese, shredded
- `40g` mozzarella cheese, shredded
- Garlic to taste
- `5` tortillas

## Steps

1. Add the chicken, taco seasoning, chicken bouillon, and green enchilada sauce (or pelati) to a slow cooker.
2. Squeeze the juice of 1 lime into the mixture.
3. Cook on low heat for about 3-4 hours.
4. After the cooking time, add the rice and cook for an additional 20 minutes or until the rice is fully cooked.
5. Add the diced jalapenos, onion, and cilantro to the mixture.
6. Stir in the Greek yogurt, cheddar, mozzarella, and garlic. Season to taste with salt.
7. Mix well to combine all the ingredients.
8. Fill the tortillas with the mixture and roll them up into burritos.
