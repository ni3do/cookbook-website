## Ingredients

- `800g` canned whole tomatoes
- `2` medium onions, diced
- `4` cloves garlic, minced
- `2 tbsp` olive oil
- `2 tbsp` butter
- `500ml` vegetable stock
- `100ml` heavy cream
- `1 tsp` sugar
- Salt and pepper to taste

## For Serving

- Crusty bread
- Fresh basil leaves

## Steps

1. Heat olive oil and butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions and cook until softened, about 8 minutes.

2. Add garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.

3. Add tomatoes (with their juices), vegetable stock, and sugar. Bring to a simmer.

4. Let simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

5. Use an immersion blender to puree until smooth. Stir in cream.

6. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot with crusty bread and fresh basil.
