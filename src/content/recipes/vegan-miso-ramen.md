## Ingredients
- ½ onion  
- 1½ garlic cloves  
- `15g` ginger  
- ½ chili  
- `100g` fresh shiitake mushrooms (or 60g dried)  
- `2` EL sesame oil  
- `2` EL soy sauce  
- 1½ EL mirin (or 60ml)  
- `0.9l` vegetable broth  
- `15g` light miso paste  
- `100g` unsweetened soja drink  

## Steps
1. Grate onion, garlic and ginger.  
2. Quarter the shiitake mushrooms. If using dried ones, hydrate first with boiling water for 15 minutes.  
3. Put sesame oil in a big pan, add onion, garlic and ginger, and sauté for 2 minutes.  
4. Add the chili, ⅓ of the mushrooms, soy sauce and mirin. Steam for 1 minute.  
5. Add the broth and let it boil. Let cook for 30 minutes on low heat.
