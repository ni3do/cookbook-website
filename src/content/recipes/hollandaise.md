## Ingredients
- `2` egg yolks  
- `80g` butter  
- `1` teaspoon dijon mustard  
- some lemon juice  
- some salt  
- pinch of pepper  

## Steps
1. Melt the butter.  
2. Add egg yolks, mustard, lemon juice, pepper, and salt together and blend quickly.  
3. While blending, stream in the melted butter slowly.
