## Ingredients

- `200g` rice cake
- `115g` tofu
- `2` carrots
- `1` pack Tteokbokki sauce
- `100g` champignons
- `30ml` cream

## Steps

1. Boil water and pour over rice cakes.
2. Let sit for 10 minutes.
3. Saut√© carrots and tofu in a pan.
4. Add champignons and cook briefly.
5. Add soaked rice cakes and Tteokbokki sauce.
6. Add cream and cook shortly.
